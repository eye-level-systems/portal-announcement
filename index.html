What is Eye Level Portal?

Eye Level Portal is our new integrated login method, powered by Okta, that combines all our major systems.

The Portal will combine access to the following platforms:

  • Key&Manager: Learning Center Management System
  • Learning Room: Eye Level member's e-Learning platform
  • Eye Level University: Our internal e-learning site using TalentLMS
  • Google Workspace: Our suite of communication tools, by Google, for select FC's and regional staff
  • Admin sites: for myeyelevel.com and Learning Room
  • Eye Level Help Center: A new support ticketing system
  • Many More Systems: Based on your role (available apps in the manual)

What Do You Need to Do?

  1. Update your email: You may soon be contacted to update our database with your work and personal email. Your work email address will be your Portal ID/username and your personal email will only be used for recovery.
  2. Provide Consent: When you receive it, review our consent form for collecting, processing, and transfer of Personal Data.
  3. Activate Account: You'll receive an activation email from Eye Level Portal / Okta. Your username, as stated above, will be your work email address.
  4. Password Setup: Ensure your password meets new security standards.
  5. Initial Login: Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) using the mobile app, Okta Verify (downloadable from App Store or Google Play Store).
  6. Explore the Portal: Familiarize yourself with the dashboard.

How to Get Help

If you encounter any issues during our launch process or have general questions, you can reach out via:

  • Email: Eye Level Help Desk
  • Manual: https://bit.ly/EyeLevelPortal
  • Regional/Country Eye Level Office: Contact your local Eye Level franchisor
  • Eye Level Help Center: Access via Eye Level Portal dashboard
