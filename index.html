What is Eye Level Portal?

Eye Level Portal is our new integrated login system that combines access to the following platforms:

  • Key&Manager: Learning Center Management System
  • Learning Room: Eye Level member's e-Learning platform
  • Eye Level University: Our internal e-learning site using TalentLMS
  • Google Workspace: Our suite of communication tools, by Google, for select FC's and regional staff
  • Admin sites: for myeyelevel.com and Learning Room
  • Eye Level Help Center: A new support ticketing system
  • Many More Systems: Based on your role

Benefits of Eye Level Portal

Single Sign-On

One username and password for all platforms

Enhanced Security

Multi-factor authentication (MFA)

Unified Policies

Standardized security policies

Easier Onboarding

Streamlined process for new users

What Do You Need to Do?

  1. Update your email: You may soon be contacted to update our database with your work and personal email.
  2. Consent to Privacy Policy: When you receive it, review and consent to our privacy policy.
  3. Initial Login: Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  4. Password Update: Ensure your password meets new security standards.
  5. Explore the Portal: Familiarize yourself with the dashboard.
