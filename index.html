What is Eye Level Portal?

No more remembering multiple URLs, multiples passowrds, multiples IDs, etc. Eye Level Portal is our new integrated login method, powered by Okta, that combines all our login accounts for our major systems.

The Portal will combine login to the following platforms:

  • Key&Manager: Learning Center Management System
  • Learning Room: Eye Level member's e-Learning platform
  • Eye Level University: Our internal e-learning site using TalentLMS
  • Google Workspace: Our suite of communication tools, by Google, for select FC's and regional staff
  • Admin sites: for myeyelevel.com and Learning Room
  • Eye Level Help Center: A new support ticketing system
  • Many More Systems: Depending on where and who you are, you'll have more apps available (refer to list in the manual)

What is going to change?

The Eye Level Portal introduces a universal login page for all our staff and instructors, streamlining access to our various systems. The Portal will significantly improve your experience with Eye Level systems while maintaining robust security. Let's explore the new steps for onboarding/offboarding different user groups:

For Learning Centers (K&M)

  • One-step process to create new user accounts and instructor profiles
  • Consent forms sent to both primary and secondary emails
  • Instant access to Learning Room and Key&Manager after self-activation (menu permissions still need to be granted)
  • Simple offboarding by filling out resignation date in K&M

Every person now requires their own Eye Level Portal account - no more reusing accounts!

For Other Users (GD)

  • Initial registration still required in Global Dreams (ERP); Information synced to Key&Manager every 6 hours
  • Consent forms sent to both primary and secondary emails
  • Immediate access (excluding admin sites) to relevant apps after setup
  • Simple offboarding by filling out resignation date in GD

No more registering in multiple services - one registration process for all!

Note: Learning center closures and ownership transfers require special procedures. Please contact support for assistance.

What Do You Need to Do?

Prepare yourself by knowing who needs access to Eye Level systems. You won't be able to share accounts anymore - so each staff/instructor will now need their own account. Previously this might have been difficult because admins for various systems had to register a user one at a time. However, going forward, registering a new account will be a breeze.

  1. Update your email: You may soon be contacted by an Eye Level regional staff member to update them with you and your staff/instructor's work and personal email. Your work email address will be your Portal ID/username and your personal email will only be used for recovery.
  2. Provide Consent: When you receive it, review our consent form for collecting, processing, and transfer of Personal Data.
  3. Activate Account: You'll receive an activation email from Eye Level Portal / Okta. Your username, as stated above, will be your work email address.
  4. Password Setup: Ensure your password meets new security standards.
  5. Initial Login: Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) using the mobile app, Okta Verify (downloadable from App Store or Google Play Store).
  6. Explore the Portal: Familiarize yourself with the dashboard.
